Fixing Warped or Sticking Doors the Right Way

More Than Just an Inconvenience

That dragging door? It’s not just annoying—it could be a sign of moisture damage, frame shifting, or poor installation. We inspect the root cause and provide repairs that go beyond sanding the edge and hoping for the best.

Clean Fixes, No Guesswork

From realigning hinges to shaving warps and replacing damaged panels, we restore doors to smooth operation without tearing apart your space. Expect tight fits, clean lines, and long-lasting results—no band-aid repairs.

Residential or Commercial

Whether it's a bedroom door that’s swelling or an office entry that's grinding against the frame, we handle repairs quickly and professionally. You don’t need a new door—you just need it fixed properly.
